What is Nvidia Blackwell Platform

Q. What is Blackwell platform for Nvidia. How it will help. When it will generate revenue?


The Nvidia Blackwell platform is a new data center platform that includes the fifth generation MVLink, multi-GPU spine, and new InfiniBand and Ethernet switches. It is designed to support various data center needs, from hyperscale to enterprise training to inference, and offers compatibility with different technologies like x86 CPUs, Ethernet, and InfiniBand networking. Blackwell will be available in over 100 OEM systems, supporting fast adoption across different customer types and data center environments. It is expected to start generating revenue in the first year of shipment.
